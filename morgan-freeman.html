    Early Life

    Morgan Freeman was born on June 1, 1937, and he is currently 86 years old. He hails from Memphis, Tennessee, and his zodiac sign is Gemini. His mother was Mamie Edna (née Revere). She lived from 1912 to 2000 and was a teacher. Likewise, his father was Morgan Porterfield Freeman. His father lived from July 6, 1915, to April 27, 1961, and he was a barber. The cause of his father’s demise was cirrhosis.

    Further, he has three older siblings. Some of his ancestors are Songhai and Tuareg people of Niger whereas some were slaves who migrated from North Carolina to Mississippi. The DNA analysis also suggested that over 1/4th of his African ancestors came from the area that stretches from present-day Senegal to Liberia. Whereas, the remaining 3/4th of his African ancestors came from the Congo-Angola region.

    Regarding his educational journey, he studied at Broad Street High School. His school’s building now serves as Threadgill Elementary School and is located in Greenwood, Mississippi. At the young age of 12 years, he was the winner of a statewide drama competition. Further, playing the lead role in a school play, he made his acting debut at the young age of 9 years. Later on, he studied theatre arts in Los Angeles.

    Morgan Freeman – Career

    Professionally, Morgan Freeman is a multi-talented personality in the Hollywood industry. More precisely, he is a remarkable actor, narrator, and director. People recognize him for his appearances in various films as well as his distinctive deep voice. Moreover, he has received many awards including Screen Actors Guild Award, Golden Globe Award, and Academy Award among others.

    The actor gained prominence in the 1970s. This was for his role in The Electric Company. Soon, he starred in Coriolanus and Julius Caesar. Both of them are Shakespearean plays while the former earned the actor an Obie Award.

    In the year 1987, the actor had a breakout role in Street Smart. In this film, he played the role of a hustler. For this role, he received an Academy Award nomination for the category “Best Supporting Actor”. The actor gained further prominence with projects like Glory, Lean on Me, and Driving Miss Daisy. He was involved in these projects all in the year 1989.

    Morgan Freeman – Personal Life

    Morgan Freeman is single at present. His first wife was Jeanette Adair Bradshaw. The two were married from October 22, 1967, until November 18, 1979. Subsequently, he married his 2nd wife – Myrna Colley-Lee – on June 16, 1984. However, they separated in December 2007, eventually divorcing on September 15, 2010.

    He welcomed his son Alfonso on September 13, 1959, with Loletha Adkins. Then with his 1st wife, he welcomed his daughter Deena. Later on, he adopted Deena’s daughter, his granddaughter – Edena Hines. Sadly, his granddaughter was murdered on August 16, 2015, at the young age of 33 years old.

    His other children are daughter Morgana and son Saifoulaye. Morgana is his daughter from his 1st marriage whereas Saigoulaye’s mother is not mentioned to the public.

Smeargle (Japanese: ドーブル Doble) is a Normal-type Pokémon introduced in Generation II. It is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon. Smeargle is best known for its ability to use the move Sketch, which permanently copies the last move used by the opponent. Due to this, it can legitimately have any move in the Pokémon games except for Chatter, Struggle, and Shadow moves — though it can learn Dark Void and Hyperspace Fury, it cannot use those moves as they can only be used by specific Pokémon.

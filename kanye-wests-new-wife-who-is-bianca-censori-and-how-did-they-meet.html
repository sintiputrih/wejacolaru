    Kanye West’s New Wife: Who is Kanye West?

    Who is Bianca Censori?

    Bianca Censori is an architectural designer who works for Kanye West’s brand Yeezy.

    According to her LinkedIn profile, she has been in the role since November 2020.

    She has a bachelor’s degree in architecture from the University of Sydney and a master’s degree in architecture from the University of Melbourne.

    She also speaks English, Italian, and Spanish.

    Censori has been involved in several projects for Yeezy, including the design of the Yeezy Gap store in New York City, which opened in June 2023.

    She has also worked on the design of West’s ranch in Wyoming, where he plans to build a futuristic city called Yeezy City.

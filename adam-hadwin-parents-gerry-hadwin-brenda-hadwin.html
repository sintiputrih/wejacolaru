    Hadwin began his professional career immediately after graduating from college. His first professional victory came on the Vancouver Golf Tour at the Ledgeview Open (VGT). He went on to win four VGT tournaments in 2009, including the Golden Ear’s Open, the Johnston Meier Insurance Open, and the RBC Invitational Pro-am, establishing himself against Western Canada’s finest pros. Hadwin took first place in a Gateway Tour Winter Series Sponsorship event in 2009.

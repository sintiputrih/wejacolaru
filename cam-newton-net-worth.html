    Cam Newton | Net Worth, NFL Contract & Total NFL Earnings

    Net Worth

    American football player Cam Newton’s net worth as of December 2023 is a staggering $75 million.

    Newton has earned an enormous amount of wealth from his successful NFL career and lucrative endorsement deals.

    He has played for two teams in the NFL to date, earning quite an astonishing amount.

    NFL Contracts

    In the 2011 NFL Draft Newton was picked first overall by Carolina Panthers.

    The same year he signed four years $22,025,498 rookie contract with the team.

    With the total guarantee of the same amount, Newton earned an average annual salary of $5,506,375 and $14,518,544 as a signing bonus.

    After the rookie contract ended in 2015, Cam immediately signed an extension with the Panthers.

    This time the five years contract was worth a staggering $103.8 million.

    It included $22.5 million in signing bonus, $20.76 million in average annual salaries, $41 million guaranteed at signing, and a $60 million total guarantee.

    Similarly, on June 28, 2020, Cam signed one year $1.75 million contract with New England Patriots.

    This contract included $1,750,000 annual salary, $550,000 guaranteed at sign and $550,000 total guaranteed.

    Patriots extended Cam’s stay on the team with one year, $5.1 million contract.

    The deal included a $2 million signing bonus, $5.1 million in salaries, $3.5 million guaranteed at signing, and another $3.5 million total guaranteed.

    In August 2021 Patriots released Cam. He eventually signed one year $6 million deal with his former team Carolina Panthers.

    This one-year contract included a $2.25 million signing bonus, $6 million in salaries, $4.5 million guaranteed at signing, and $4.5 million total guaranteed.

    Total NFL Earnings

    So how much has Cam Newton earned from NFL so far?

    So in total, Cam Newton has bagged $133,545,071 over his 11 seasons in the NFL.

    Out of total $68,673,620 came from salary, $41,268,544 on signing bonuses, 9,656,250 in roster bonuses, $1,600,000 in workout bonuses, $10,000,000 in option bonuses, and $2,346,657 in incentives.

    Similarly, he earned $127,393,521 with Carolina Panthers and $6,151,550 with New England Patriots.

    Likewise, Cam paid a total fine of $58,796 throughout his time with the Panthers.

    The death of Australian musician Lil Bo Weep shocked her fans and her family members. Lil Bo was struggling with her “demons” and on 5 March, she passed away. Here are a few things you need to learn about her parents and her siblings. We also address her miscarriage and her relationship.

    Keep reading to learn more about Lil Bo.

    Lil Bo Weep Cause Of Death

    Australian singer Lil Bo Weep has sadly passed away according to the report of The Sun. She died on 5 March 2022 but the cause of her death is yet to be revealed. The rapper’s father confirmed her death via a Facebook post.

    He heartbreakingly wrote, “This weekend we lost the fight for my daughter’s life against depression, trauma, PTSD, and drug addiction that we have been fighting since we got her back from America through emergency repatriation DFAT but broken. She fought hard against her demons as we all did a side by side next to her and picked up the broken pieces over and over again but she could not fight anymore and we lost her. ”

    “As her dad, I am proud of her beyond words as she is my hero, my daughter, and my best friend that I love so so much She is no longer hurting now with the universe wanting their angel back,” he added.

    He concluded, “A big part of me is lost at the moment but I ask respectfully that my close friends try not to ring me until I get some way through this. ALWAYS IN MY HEART. I LOVE MY WINNIE”.

    Tribute for the rapper and her grieving family started pouring in on the internet.

    Canadian singer Alice Glass said she “loved” Lil Bo’s music. She continued, “Rip Lil Bo Weep. “First heard ‘I wrote this song 4 you’ and loved your music immediately. “I’m so sorry you suffered I wish this Angel was still here and never met anyone that would hurt you.”

    Family and friends of her father also posted on the FB wall and the post and paid their tribute.

    Back in February 2022, Lil Bo had posted on her Instagram, “I love being alive, wow its just so much fun waking up every day knowing that we are in a giant loop of time that never ends and we are all just filling up that time with distractions until our vessels eventually start to give out and be reborn into the same cycle”.

    During an interview with HighThoughtsTV, Lil Bo shared that when she died she told her friends that people to look at her in her coffin and her body held her life stories.

    Lil Bo Weep Parents

    Lil Bo Weep was born to her father Matthew Schofield (@matthew.green.946179) and her mother Vic Jeanopoullis (@vic.jeanopoullis). Her parents are separated, not known for how long. However, her mother married her husband Simon Stewart (@simon.stewart1).

    Simon and Vic have two kids together below the age of 5, and the older child (Lil Bo’s sister) has appeared on her IG a few times. Back in 2018, her mother featured Weep on her Facebook when her mother graduated college.

    Did Lil Bo Weep Have A Boyfriend?

    In her last post, Lil Bo took to Instagram and shared that she was grieving over having a miscarriage. She shared that she was crying the whole day. She also shared about her plans to do something in remembrance of her departed daughter.

    Lil Bo captioned the post, “btw I’m NOT high, I am heavily dosed on Seroquel to avoid any psychosis from my cptsd. not only have I been mourning my child, but I have also recently found out my fertility has been affected by growing up with a severe eating disorder. If you so so so any sort of a ninja Cerner I recommend to think about what you’re doing to yourself because I may never be able to have a full-term pregnancy or have a child of my own due to the way I treated my body.”

    There is no evidence of her having a boyfriend at the time of her death.

    Lil Bo Weep had previously dated musician Steven Moses. On a GoFundMe page, she shared a few details about her relationship. She shared that in January 2020, she flew to LA on tour met up with Steven who had cheated on her and abused her throughout the year.

    Steven according to the post introduced her to heroin and not long after she forced him into rehab. Then they got into fentanyl.

    Lil Bo shared about supporting him but he used her. In November, she got pregnant and he would continuously smoke fentanyl around her knowing she was pregnant. She was on a brink of committing suicide till her dad flew her back on an emergency flight during the time of COVID-19.

    She spent over $24K and even lost her house while with him.

    Lil Bo Weep Net Worth

    As of 2022, Lil Bo had a net worth under $100 thousand. She had previously shared about her financial struggles. She was a very popular music artist with $227K monthly listeners on her Spotify account. Her song “not ok but it’s ok” has 12 million plays on the platform.

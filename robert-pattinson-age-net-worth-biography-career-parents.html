    Pattinson is an English actor from London, England. He is best known for starring in films like Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire and Twilight.

    The franchise Twilight consists of five movies between 2008 to 2012 which grossed a combined total of $3.3 billion all over the world.

    Let’s learn more about his private life, family, wife, net worth, and other significant details about him.

    Robert Pattinson age

    He is 35 years old.

    Robert Pattinson’s early life

    Robert Douglas Thomas Pattinson was born in London, England, on May 13, 1986. His mother’s name is Clare, and she used to work for a modeling agency, while his father, Richard, used to import classic cars from America.

    Furthermore, he had a tremendous desire to be a performer despite being extremely timid since he was a child. The singer Lizzy Pattinson, Robert’s older sister, also served as an inspiration.

    Robert Pattinson height

    6 ft (1.85m)

    Robert Pattinson education

    He completed his early education at The Tower House School in London, England, United Kingdom. He later enrolled himself at the Harrodian School in London, England, the United Kingdom from where he completed his further studies.

    After that, he attended a nearby university in the United States and earned his degree there. He was always far more interested in performing and extracurricular activities than in academics from the very beginning of his life.

    Robert Pattinson siblings

    He has two siblings. He has two sisters named Victoria Pattinson, and Lizzy Pattinson.

    Robert Pattinson parents

    Robert Pattinson’s Parents are Richard and Clare Pattinson. His father’s name is Mr. Richard Pattinson Raymond, who is a vintage car dealer by profession.

    Robert Pattinson wife

    Robert Pattinson’s marital status is married. In the year 2018, he married Suki Waterhouse who is an English singer, actress, and model.

    Robert Pattinson career

    Ring of the Nibelungs, a television movie, marked Robert’s television debut in 2004. The year after, he played a minor part in the movie Vanity Fair.

    Robert then reunited with the Twilight cast in 2009 for the sequel of Twilight: New Moon. The movie was directed by Chris Wietz and it was released on 16th November 2009. Same as its prequel, the movie grossed around $700 million all over the globe.

    In addition to appearing in various other films throughout the production of the Twilight series, Robert played the major roles in Love and Distruct and Remember Me, two other romantic comedies.

    Robert Pattinson net worth

    As of October 2022, Robert Pattinson net worth is estimated to be $100 million. He has earned this massive net worth through his successful acting career.

    He has been working in the field since 2004. He rose to fame after starring as Cedric Diggory in the 2005 film Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.

    For his work in both high-budget and low-budget movies, Pattinson has been recognized as one of the highest-paid actors in the world. He was listed on Forbes’ Celebrity 100 list and was one of the 100 most powerful people in the world according to Time magazine.

    SUMMER Walker is an American R&B star, with her latest album, Still Over It becoming a hit.

    Here is what we know about the star, originally from Atlanta, Georgia.

    Who is Summer Walker? 

    Summer Marjani Walker was born on April 11, 1996.

    For two years, she made ends meet by working as a cleaner and a stripper in her home city.

    During this time, she taught herself how to play guitar by watching tutorials on YouTube.  

    But talent and luck combined, a woman who works as the studio manager of a label, LVRN, discovered her.

    Summer signed with Love Renaissance and Interscope Records.

    In November 2019, her debut album Over It dropped, with the biggest first week for any female R&B singer since Beyonce released Lemonade.

    She and the 30-year-old rapper and producer partner, London on da Track, welcomed their daughter in March 2021, after being together for two years.

    How can I listen to her music? 

    Still Over It is Summer's second studio album by American, as released on November 5, 2021, by LVRN and Interscope.

    It can be listened to on Spotify. 

    The album is a follow-up to Walker's 2019 debut album, Over It.

    On the second anniversary of her debut album, Walker announced the release of Still Over It through a video teaser. 

    The album includes 20 tracks, featuring collaborations with Cardi B, City Girls member JT, SZA, Ari Lennox, Lil Durk, Pharrell Williams, Omarion, and Ciara. 

    The lead single, Ex For A Reason, was released on October 15, 2021.

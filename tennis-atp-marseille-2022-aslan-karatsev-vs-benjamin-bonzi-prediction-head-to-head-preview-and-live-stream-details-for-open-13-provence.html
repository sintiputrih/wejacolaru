    Aslan Karatsev vs Benjamin Bonzi: Preview

    Karatsev had a terrific start to the season, winning his first title, the Sydney International, in straight sets 6-3 6-3 against Andy Murray. Since then, the Russian hasn’t been able to match that level of performance in any of his subsequent events. In the third round of the Australian Open, he was defeated by Adrian Mannarino. Following that, he lost two first-round matches in a row at the Maharashtra Open and the ATP Rotterdam. He has only played one match in this event, winning 6-3 7-6 against Holger Rune to advance to the quarter-finals.

    Bonzi, on the other hand, is off to a slow start this season. In the first warmup match before the Australian Open in Adelaide, he lost in the first round, and in the second, he was eliminated in straight sets in the round of 16. He was beaten out of the Australian Open second round by Karen Khachanov, the 28th seed. He was eliminated in the first round of the next tournament in France. But, shortly before the open 13 province event, he made a comeback at the challenger competition in France. So far in this tournament, he’s had a respectable showing. He advanced to the quarter-finals by defeating Pierre-Hugues Herbert in the round of 16.

    Aslan Karatsev vs Benjamin Bonzi: Head to Head

    This will be the first meeting between the duo, so their current head to head stands at 0-0.

    Aslan Karatsev vs Benjamin Bonzi: Prediction

    Because the two have never played against each other before, it’s difficult to make a prediction because neither of them is familiar with the other’s gaming style. Karatsev, however, has had a slightly stronger year and is riding high in this event. In the quarter-finals, the fourth seed is set to tackle Bonzi. Bonzi is likely to put up a fight.

    Prediction: Karatsev to win in three sets.

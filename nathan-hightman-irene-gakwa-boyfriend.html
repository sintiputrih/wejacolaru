    Who Is Nathan Hightman?

    Irene Gakwa, a Kenyan native, went to the US in 2019 to seek a profession in healthcare. She met Nathan “Nate” J. Hightman on Craiglist. Without telling her family, she went to Gillette, Wyoming, with him in July 2021. Prior to meeting Nate, her older brothers, Kennedy Wainaina and Chris Munga, both of Idaho, claimed to have seen their sister practically every weekend. Chris allegedly stated, “I feel like Nate is the one who kind of pushed her away from the family.”

    Irene’s brothers claimed they had only met Nate a few times and were unaware of the couple’s relationship status. Chris said, “I just didn’t like him from the moment I met him, but I never told her. He just seemed troublesome to me. Additionally, he claimed they had “a rocky relationship,” alleging she had misled them about travelling to Arizona for a warmer environment when, in fact, she was moving in with him. The brothers called a close acquaintance after Irene started acting strangely in late February 2022 and learnt the truth.

    Irene’s family learned she had deleted her WhatsApp account on March 8 and her phone number two days later, so they reported her missing to the Gillette Police Department on March 20, 2022. Nate apparently stated he hadn’t seen her since late February when he was visited by a police officer the same day reg arding a connected but unrelated criminal matter. He claimed that on one occasion in late February, she returned home, put her clothes into two plastic bags, and left the house in a dark SUV. However, after he allegedly refused to return her paperwork, her family began to question him.

    According to reports, Gillette police engaged in a wild goose chase while looking into Nate’s allegations that Ireme had stolen money to buy plane tickets to Kenya. However, the police disregarded the accusations after a thorough investigation revealed no merit. In April 2022, after witnesses alerted authorities to a grey or silver Subaru Crosstrek with Idaho licence plates, Nate was formally designated as a person of interest. Kennedy falsely claimed that he had burned something in a 55-gallon oil drum while driving a vehicle that was similar to his own.

    Where Is Nathan Hightman Now?

    On May 10, 2022, at around 3:30 pm, Nate was taken into custody by the police on suspicion of two felonies for theft, one felonious for using a credit card without authorization, and two felonies for crimes against intellectual property. According to police sources, he changed her online bank password after she vanished and then took $3,700, spent $3,230 on her credit card. Nate, however, insisted he did it in order to force her to get in touch with him if she needed financial assistance.

He was given a suspended two- to three-year sentence for offences against intellectual property, along with a three- to six-year jail sentence for felony theft and using a credit card without authorization. At his sentencing in June 2023, he was also had to pay $13,200 in penalties, fees, and restitution.

    How Many Siblings Does Olivia Culpo Have? Olivia Culpo has four siblings Sophia Culpo, Peter Culpo, Aurora Culpo, and Gus Culpo.

    They had a cultural upbringing as her mother was a teacher herself. They have a classical music background and are of Italian descent.

    The Cranston natives live in Los Angeles. Olivia has several restaurants with her father, cousin Josha Culpo, and business partner Justin Dalton-Ameen.

    Olivia, Sophia, and Aurora are the trios of the reality TV show The Cuplo Sisters. It aired in November 2020 on TLC.

    They have ventured into multiple businesses and gained titles, including reality TV stars, models, entrepreneurs, and businesswomen. They released a clothing collaboration with Macy’s x Inc.

    They all have been a part of the eagle-eyed public since the launch of the TLC series titled The Culpo Sisters. The family dynamics can be seen on the show.

    Olivia Culpo Brothers

    Olivia Culpo brothers are Gus Culpo and Pete Culpo. Gus is younger than her, and Pete is older than her.

    Gus Culpo

    Augustus aka Gus Culpo is a pianist. Gus also played baseball during college life as Pitcher.

    Gus Culpo age is 27 years old, and he was born on 17 August 1995.

    Sports runs through his veins. his uncle Mike Culpo played college basketball. Gus was a member of three state-championship basketball teams.

    Gus studied at the University of Southern California as an economics major. He attended Bishop Hendricken HS  and Orange Coast College while playing basketball as team captain.

    The athlete also attended the University of Pittsburgh, Rhode Island, and Orange Coast College while continuing his journey in baseball. Gus is currently associated with Rhode Island Rams.

    Pete Culpo

    Peter is a bassist in Boston. Peter is also her father’s name so he is better known as only Pete.

    Pete Culpo age is 35 years old as per Bandmix. He has contributed 15 years of his life to music and has played over 100 gigs.

    He is a member of the cover band Future Dads five members based in Boston. He is also a member of the 90s alternative tribute band, Be Kind, Rewind. His stage name is Pedro.

    The bassist attended the University of Rhode Island and earned a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.), Business/Corporate Communications degree.

    Peter married Katie Trainor in June 2019 after nine years of dating. They recently welcomed a daughter in 2021.

    Olivia Culpo Sisters

    Olivia Culpo sisters are Sophia Culpo and Aurora Culpo. They attended an all-girls Catholic school.

    They live together in Los Angeles. Sophia and Aurora got fame through their sister Olivia who was crowned Miss USA and then Miss Universe in 2012.

    The sisters teamed up as a trio for a fashion line and now a television show called The Culpo Sisters. In an interview with ENews, Olivia hinted the show might be returning for season 2 on HBO Max.

    The show covers their relationship changes, personal life, breakups, major life decisions, busy career, and dramas over dresses and insecurities sisters have.

    Sophia Culpo

    Sophia Culpo is a nutritionist. She has a degree in Bachelor’s degree in Nutrition and Public Health from Boston University.

    Sophia Culpo age is 26 years old; she was born on 15 November 1996. Her height is 5 feet and 6 inches.

    Besides her career as a nutritionist, she is a professional model. Sophia is a brand ambassador of Savage X Fenty, USANA Health Services and Beautycounter

    She also promotes her nutrition knowledge for her career and healthy eating habits. She is also into business and recently ventured to sell products through Shop My.

    Sophia dated NFL player Braxton Berrios from 2021 to 2023. The former couple lived together on East Coast and moved back to California.

    Aurora Culpo

    Aurora Culpo is a digital content creator. She is the co-host of the Barely Filtered podcast.

    Aurora is 34 years old. The oldest Culpo sister was born on 4 April 1989 under the sign of Aries. She described herself as Bossy as Trade.

    Culpo was married to Michael “Mikey” Bortone for three years. She shares joint custody of her children Remi Berkeley and Solei Marie.

    The influencer has a Master’s degree in teaching. She formerly worked with behavior analysis to work specifically with kids with Autism and developmental delays. She graduated from Fairfield University in Connecticut.

    In November, an episode of The Culpo Sisters revealed some bitter truths about a contract. Mikey was allowed to sleep with other women once a year, but after years of marriage Aurora wanted to change it, but he disagreed.

    Olivia Culpo Parents

    Olivia Culpo parents are Susan Curran and Peter Culpo. Olivia was born and raised in Cranston, Rhode Island.

    The Culpo family owns multiple restaurants in Rhode Island and Boston. They rose to stardom with their kid’s success globally.

    Her grandfather, John Cuplo was an accordion player. He had done several stage performances including at the prestigious Carnegie Hall.

    In an interview with TLC, they revealed they were unsure of their kid’s occupations. They are still unaware of what exactly Olivia does for a living.

    Olivia Culpo Mom Susan Curran Culpo

    Olivia Culpo mom Susan Curran Culpo is a classically trained musician. She played viola for the Boston Pops, Rhode Island Philharmonic Orchestra, and Boston Symphony.

    She studied music under Leonard Bernstein and is a professional violinist. Susan is a homemaker and manages her household responsibilities. She has Irish ancestry.

    Susan performed with Marlana VanHoose during the Best Buddies Mother’s Day Brunch Hosted by Vanessa & Gina Hudgens on May 12, 2018, in Malibu.

    In 2016, the actress shared a picture of Susan helping some kids cross the road as a Mother’s Day tribute post.

    In March 2023, the 30-year-old walked and attended the 11th Annual Endometriosis Foundation of America’s Blossom Ball in New York with Susan. They raised funds for education and health in needs.

    Olivia Culpo Dad Peter Culpo

    Olivia Culpo dad Peter Culpo is a restaurateur at Union & Main. The restaurant was featured in the show The Culpo Sisters.

    The father-daughter duo is also the partner of the Back 40 restaurant in North Kingston. Culpo also owns a restaurant, Lulu’s Allston named after Aurora’s nickname.

    Peter Culpo restaurants are “Black Oak Kitchen + Drink”s on Coventry. Parish Cafe, Woody’s Grill, Tap in the Fenway, and The Hill Tavern in Beacon Hill are other restaurants.

    Culpo has more than three decades of experience in the hospitality business. He is a leading personality in the food and beverage industry.

    In an interview with Forbes, the model shared that Peter taught her the significance of being as fiscally conservative as possible. He taught her to invest and think ahead from an early age.

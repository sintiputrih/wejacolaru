    New Mattel WWE Legends Prototype Images Revealed and More! (Photos)

    Mattel and WWE teamed up yesterday to share upcoming images of a lot of WWE toys, such as Legends Series 9! New images of this next wave of WWE Legends figures surfaced yesterday on WWE.com! This set includes:

    26 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Mattel and WWE teamed up yesterday to share upcoming images of a lot of WWE toys, such as Legends Series 9!

    Photo by Moses Robinson/Getty Images

    WWE Legends Series 9

    New images of this next wave of WWE Legends figures surfaced yesterday on WWE.com! This set includes:

    • Ted Dibiase (plus Chase variant)
    • The Undertaker
    • Tatanka
    • Nikolai Volkoff

    These are Target exclusive and are expected to hit shelves in 2021. These will most likely release around quarter two of next year if I had to guess.

    WWE Wrekkin Slamcycles

    New vehicles in the Wrekkin’ toy line were revealed as well. This time we get two motorcycles that break apart into pieces as well as come with a unique action figure! These include:

    • Undertaker
    • Drew McIntyre

    WWE WREKKIN SLAMCYCLES: pic.twitter.com/3eVubk5IE0

    WWE Championship Showdown

    Also revealed were the new WWE Championship Showdown action figures and belt that accompanies them. These figures look to be replacing Battle Packs as each set comes with side plates for the wrestlers within the pack that can then go on the role play belt. This first set includes:

    • Roman Reigns vs. Finn Bálor
    • Alexa Bliss vs. Sasha Banks
    • Jeff Hardy vs. Undertaker
    • WWE Championship Showdown WWE Championship Belt

    You can currently pre-order these on RingsideCollectibles.com where you can with discount code at checkout!

    WWE Top Talent Series 2021

    The latest Basic Top Picks Series was revealed as well. This set includes:

    • John Cena
    • The Rock
    • Braun Strowman
    • Roman Reigns

    You can currently pre-order these on RingsideCollectibles.com as well where you can with discount code at checkout!

    WWE TOP PICKS 2021 SERIES: Pre-order now on @RingsideC and SAVE 10% with code MBG at checkout! pic.twitter.com/2PCLzS8BVy

    WWE Elite 80

    Final prototype images of WWE Elite 80 were also shown. These are already in-stock over on Ringside if you desire to pick them up. We will be reviewing these very soon, too. The set includes:

    • Ivar
    • Erik
    • Bayley
    • Ricochet
    • Kevin Owens
    • Kyle O’Reilly (plus Chase variant)
    • Rocky Johnson (Collector’s Edition)

    The Ringside Exclusive Walter was also revealed as were other lineups we’ve reviewed already for you.

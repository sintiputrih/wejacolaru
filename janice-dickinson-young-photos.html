    When it comes to defining success, Janice Dickinson won’t settle for just one title. The self-proclaimed ‘first supermodel’ has dominated the worlds of fashion, television, and business with her unapologetic flair and unwavering confidence.

    She was a regular cover girl and catwalk queen during the ’70s and ’80s. Back a generation later, she made her mark as a judge on America’s Next Top Model (2003-06). In 2005, she opened her own modeling agency, which was featured on the reality series, The Janice Dickinson Modeling Agency, from 2006 to 2008.

    Continuing her run in the reality world, Janice has appeared on Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here!, Janice & Abbey, Celebrity Rehab with Dr. Drew, and Celebrity Big Brother 16. She’s written three books: No Lifeguard on Duty (2002), Everything About Me Is Fake… And I’m Perfect (2004), and Check Please! Dating, Mating, and Extricating (2006).

    Seriously?! Taylor Lautner has had six-pack abs literally since he was a kid. The Twilight actor, 24, shared a throwback photo of himself as a little boy on the beach with his martial arts instructor on Thursday, August 4, and he was impressively jacked.

    “#tbt beach flexin 99′ #summerbod,” the Scream Queens actor captioned the adorable photo. In the snap, little 7-year-old Lautner flexes his guns while showcasing his rock-hard abs.

    The actor grew up practicing martial arts. In a video posted to YouTube in December 2011, a younger Lautner can be seen doing backflips during a demonstration with his bo staff.

    Let’s also not forget when the Twilight hunk defended then-girlfriend Taylor Swift after Kanye West’s infamous 2009 VMAs faux pas. Lautner appeared on Saturday Night Live after the awards show and performed a “Swift kick” by beheading a West mannequin.

    “It taught me a lot of things at a young age: discipline, hard work. Nothing comes without hard work,” Lautner told E! News in 2010 of martial arts, revealing he started the sport at the age of 6.

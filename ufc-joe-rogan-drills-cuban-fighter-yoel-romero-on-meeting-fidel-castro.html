    Yoel Romero is one of the most astonishing fighters on this planet. ‘The Soldier Of God ‘ actively competes against young fighters in Bellator at the age of 45. With a superhero physique, Yoel Romero also had multiple heroic performances in the Octagon. However, it was not easy for Romero to reach the UFC. When he came on the Joe Rogan Experience, Yoel talked about his struggles in Cuba and how he met the controversial figure Fidel Castro.

    Coaches chose Yoel Romero to attend a specialist wrestling school based purely on his physical attributes at eight years of age. As a result, Yoel Romero went on to win the world championship and Olympic medals. He talked to Joe Rogan about meeting Fidel Castro with Joey Diaz translating. Joe Rogan asked Yoel about meeting Fidel Castro, to which Yoel replied,” I was sitting next to him as I am to you.”


    Hearing this, Rogan got chills on his body. He further questioned whether it was weird meeting Castro, to which Romero had an unusual response. According to Yoel, he saw the dictator as a human being. Rogan replied, saying, “You saw Fidel as a human?” Yoel added, “He was also the President of Cuba, so that too.“ Rogan was fairly surprised by Yoel’s views, given that Castro is very controversial.

    Yoel Romero also talked about the difficulty of defecting from Cuba, leaving his family behind. It finally paid off, as his family lives a good life in Cuba due to the monetary benefits of his MMA career. Yoel Romero will fight for the Bellator light heavyweight championship at Bellator 297.

    Yoel Romero is a certified superhuman with his performance and his freakish build

    Being 45 and actively competing in a sport for young people is not easy. Yoel Romero is a freak of nature. He competed for the title in UFC at the age of 43 against Israel Adesanya. After being cut from the UFC, Yoel did not stop. He went to Bellator and became the No.1 contender for the Bellator Light Heavyweight title.


    Joe Rogan talked about the supernatural nature of Yoel’s Physique multiple times. The physicians and doctors of the UFC have since confirmed Yoel’s superhuman status. According to Rogan, the UFC brought Romero to an optometrist after a fight to examine his orbital area.” They brought in Yoel Romero because he had a fractured orbital in one of his fights,” said Rogan. The doctor examined him and called the UFC, asking, ‘Where did you get this guy?‘. Yoel was the most unusual human being the doctor had ever seen in his 40 years of practicing medicine.

    Comments from the doctor make Romere a certified freak of nature. ‘The Soldier of God ‘ will face Vadim Nemkov for the Bellator Light Heavyweight World Championship on June 16, 2023, at Bellator 297.

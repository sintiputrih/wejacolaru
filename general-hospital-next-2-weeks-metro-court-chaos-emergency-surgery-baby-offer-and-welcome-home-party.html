    General Hospital (GH) spoilers for the next two weeks, July 3 to July 14, reveal that Drew Cain (Cameron Mathison) will immediately face a risky situation as his sentence at Pentonville begins. During the week of July 3-7, Cyrus Renault (Jeff Kober) will recruit Drew to be on his team behind bars, but Drew could face trouble whether he complies or resists.

    There’ll be a repeat on Tuesday, July 4, but the show will quickly resume with all-new episodes and plenty of shockers.

    Felicia Scorpio (Kristina Wagner) will launch the investigation Lucy Coe (Lynn Herring) pushed for, so she’ll soon uncover some surprising red flags about Martin Grey (Michael E. Knight).

    Over with Tracy Quartermaine (Jane Elliot), she set up a meeting with Diane Miller (Carolyn Hennesy).

    Tracy might explore legal options regarding “Eddie Maine” aka Ned Quartermaine (Wally Kurth) – like having him committed if possible.

    At Deception, Brook Lynn Quartermaine (Amanda Setton) will have to think fast after Lucy realizes confidential info has been stolen.

    We’ll see Brook Lynn spin a story about trying to be a more informed employee – and she’ll play dumb about knowing there were trade secrets in the files she copied.

    After Molly Lansing-Davis (currently played by Holiday Mia Kriegel) confides in Kristina Corinthos-Davis (Kate Mansi), she’ll get an offer that catches her off guard.

    Molly will mull that offer over during the week of July 10-14, so this probably involves Kristina suggesting she could have a baby for TJ Ashford (Tajh Bellow) and her sister.

    Alexis Davis (Nancy Lee Grahn) will likely think this is another one of Kristina’s wild ideas that she won’t follow through on or will end up regretting.

    Offering to have a baby for someone is a huge deal, so Alexis will deliver a warning and may worry that Kristina doesn’t know what she’s signing up for.

    Kristina will get the brush off later, so Molly may not be ready to give her an answer.

    The fact that Kristina even offers at all could leave Molly a little freaked out and distant until she processes everything.

    Next, let’s talk about Josslyn Jacks (Eden McCoy) and something she’ll witness.

    Josslyn will see a sight she disapproves of and will eventually confront Esme Prince (Avery Pohl), so she may suspect Esme is trying to steal Trina Robinson’s (Tabyana Ali) boyfriend.

    Perhaps Josslyn will spot a close moment between Esme and Spencer Cassadine (Nicholas Alexander Chavez), which could lead to her telling Esme to stop all her manipulation.

    Over with Elizabeth Baldwin (Rebecca Herbst) and Hamilton Finn (Michael Easton), they’ll get honest about their mutual feelings and hopefully decide whether they’re going to try pursuing a romance again.

    While Lucy and Felicia kick off a fun caper, Cody Bell (Josh Kelly) will do his best to get the upper hand on Selina Wu (Lydia Look).

    That sounds like a terrible idea since Selina has proven she’s not easily outwitted!

    There’ll also be a scary ordeal at the Metro Court after someone fires a shot.

    Nina Reeves (Cynthia Watros) will face some horror, but Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Benard) will apparently stay safe since his children will keep going about their business.

    Michael Corinthos (Chad Duell) will pull off a grand gesture for Willow Corinthos (Katelyn MacMullen), who’ll be able to return to the gatehouse after some positive updates at the hospital.

    Willow will even invite Nina to be part of her tiny welcome home party, but Nina will have to play nice with Carly Spencer (Laura Wright) to be included.

    As for the victim from the Metro Court, that person will cling to life at GH.

    TJ Ashford (Tajh Bellow) will help out with emergency surgery, but good news should follow.

    This could tie in with Ava Jerome (Maura West) and Austin Gatlin-Holt’s storyline (Roger Howarth), especially if Austin saves Ava from Mason Gatlin-Holt (Nathanyael Grey) and puts his own life on the line.

    Trina will get hit with some big emotions, so that may involve reconnecting with Ava after the crisis and seeing that she’s OK.

    Harrison Chase (Josh Swickard) will make an arrest before the week’s over while Dante Falconeri (Dominic Zamprogna) settles a new theory, so the PCPD will be hard at work!

    General Hospital spoilers say the next two weeks will bring some action-packed episodes, so stay tuned.

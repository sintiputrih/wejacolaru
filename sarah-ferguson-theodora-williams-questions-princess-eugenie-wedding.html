    Kids say the funniest things.

    Theodora Williams, daughter of British pop star Robbie Williams and actress Ayda Field, served as one of Princess Eugenie's bridesmaids on Friday, and the adorable (and outspoken) six-year-old couldn't help but ask the Duchess of York, Sarah Ferguson, a question when both were on the steps of St. George's Chapel.

    "Are you the Queen?" Theodora asked the mother of the bride in the clip below.

    "No," the Ferguson replied.

    Theodora then followed up with, "Are you a princess?" This prompted Ferguson to say, "Yes!" And run off.

    Perhaps the sweet girl was just implying that Ferguson looked quite regal in her emerald green dress.

    The cute moment was captured on camera just as Princess Eugenie and Jack Brooksbank's wedding ended. Fans loved every second of the exchange.

    "This is adorable on so many levels!" one commenter wrote on Twitter. "#Love that @yorkiebea has to hold them all back, @SarahTheDuchess trying not to hold things up, yet sweetly tries to answer Miss Willliams' question, also...the #Realness of Miss Williams' innocent inquiry!"

    WPA Pool//Getty Images

    Although Ferguson told Theodora that she is a princess, her real title is the Duchess of York. It was changed from Her Royal Highness the Duchess of York following her split with Prince Andrew.

    Theodora, although not royalty, goes by another name herself. According to Town and Country, she's known best to her family by the adorable nickname Teddy.

    "Happy 6th Birthday to my beautiful Teddy!!" her mom wrote on Instagram in September. "I am constantly amazed at how magic you are."

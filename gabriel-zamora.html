    Gabriel Zamora (born March 10, 1993) is an American YouTuber, Makeup Artist, Vlogger, Social Media Influencer, and Instagram Personality from Los Angeles, California. He is famous for his self-entitled YouTube where he has 856+ k subscribers.

    Furthermore, the 26-year-old artist posts numerous videos related to makeup tutorials, viral game challenges, product reviews, skincare routines and much more. He is the first male YouTuber to sign with Michelle Phan’s beauty community titled “Ipsy” as a stylist.

    Moreover, Zamora started his career in the marketing industry and took his first job in Houston. But soon he fed up from his 9 to 5 job. In addition, he also worked in stores like Rue21, Forever 21, and Zumiez in Texas.

    Later, Gabriel shifted to Los Angeles and started his profession in the beauty industry. In 2014, the makeup artist got inspired by his YouTuber idol, Patrick Starrr and shared his first YouTube makeup video.

    In fact, Lilly Ghalichi (Shahs of Sunset star) also encouraged him to pursue his career as a makeup artist. She also hired him as the social media coordinator for Lilly’s Lashes.

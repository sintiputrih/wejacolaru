    Who Was Tarik Pierce?

    As said earlier, Tarik Pierce was a resident of Washington, D.C., but he was a native of Florence, South Carolina. He worked at the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs.

    Regarding his educational background, Tarik went to Clemson University and earned his bachelor’s degree of Science in Financial Management.

    After that, he received his Masters degree in Human Resources Development from the same university. After completing his education, he worked as Program Analyst/ Human Resources Consultant at Advanced Resource Technologies, Inc.

    In the same way, Tarik served as a Workforce Development Specialist at the United States Department of Commerce. He was also a training officer at the Department of Commerce (DOC).

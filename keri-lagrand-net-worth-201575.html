    About

    Dancer and Choreographer known for her work on tours and performances with stars like Cher, Gloria Estefan, Jennifer Lopez, and Madonna, amongst countless others.

    She began dancing at the age of three and at eight began training as a Gymnast. By thirteen she would begin attending dance conventions around the US.

    Rishi Sunak Using Hammer Went Viral:

    On a recent visit to Farsley in West Yorkshire, Rishi Sunak participated in a craft session involving jewellery making. The clip that garnered attention on social media showcased the Prime Minister using the hammer in an unconventional way, with some users suggesting he struggled to handle the tool effectively. The video quickly spread across platforms, drawing reactions from users and even opposition parties.

    Rishi Sunak Hammer Video:

    Opposition parties were swift to capitalize on the viral video, using it as an opportunity to criticize Sunak’s capabilities. Labour, referencing Keir Starmer’s father being a toolmaker, shared the video with a pointed message: “Not the son of a toolmaker… Man who hammers working people can’t work a hammer.”

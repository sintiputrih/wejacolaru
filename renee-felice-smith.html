    Renée Felice Smith – TV Career

    Renée Felice Smith was introduced to the acting world when she was just 6. However, she made her actual debut in the theater with Second Stage’s film Wildflower.

    In 2010, Smith caught the role of Nell Jones in the series ‘NCIS: Los Angeles’ which she has been doing for nearly 10 years. This role of hers has turned out to be a breakthrough role.

    American professional basketball player Brandon Jennings has an estimated net worth of $16 million. He is currently playing as a point guard for National Basketball Association’s Washington Wizards.

    Initially, Jennings chose to attend University of Southern California. However, he changed his mind and decided to attend University of Arizona. After attending Oak Hill Academy, he played professional basketball with Lottomatica Roma. On July 16, 2008, he signed a $1.65 million net income guaranteed contract with the Italian Lega A’s Lottomatica Roma, After a year with the Italian Club, he declared himself eligible for the 2009 NBA Draft. Eventually, he was chosen as the 10th overall pick by the Milwaukee Bucks. This made him the first professional player to skip college, but played for a European team, then later be drafted by the NBA. After spending four seasons with the Bucks, Jennings was traded to Detroit Pistons in July 31, 2013.

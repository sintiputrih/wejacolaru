    Spike Lee is an iconic director, born on March 20, 1957 in Atlanta, GA. He is best known for his 1989 film Do the Right Thing, which earned him critical praise and an Academy Award nomination for Best Original Screenplay. He has also directed other celebrated films such as Malcolm X, Inside Man and 25th Hour, further cementing his legacy as one of the most influential directors of all time.

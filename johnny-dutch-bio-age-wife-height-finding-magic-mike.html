    Meet Johnny Dutch, a new face on the reality TV show biz who stars in a new reality game show on HBO Max that is called Finding Magic Mike.

    The new series that premiered on 16 December 2021 comes from the producers behind the Magic Mike franchise and live shows and seemed to have absorbed its anticipators with its sexy dancing content like no other.

    Now, with that being said, let us take you only through Johnny Dutch’s journey on the show and more in this ‘Johnny Dutch Bio’.

    Johnny Dutch On Finding Magic Mike

    The new series comes from the producers behind the Magic Mike franchise and live shows, it comprises seven episodes and follows a group of 10 non-professional dancers or strippers, who having lost their magic, are looking to find it along with a $100,000 prize and the opportunity to perform in the Magic Mike Live stage show in Las Vegas.

    So, Johnny came on Finding Magic Mike as a professional track and field athlete. And despite any background in dancing he quickly found his footing and stood out among the initial performers. His toned body and athleticism came in handy (of course).

    Talking about it all, Johnny shared the journey was pretty challenging and helped him face his vulnerabilities. He was quick to pick up challenging choreographies and remained attentive to the audience while performing. And without even knowing he was making his way to the season finale, where he won the title of Magic Mike as well.

    Johnny Dutch Age

    Johnny Dutch had reached the age of 32 when he got an opportunity to be a part of Finding Magic Mike in 2021.

    Does Johnny Dutch Have A Wife?

    Johnny Dutch did not seem to have been married as of the time of this writing. Even if he had been married, he chose to hide his wife from the prying eyes of media as well as his fans.

    Of course, Johnny did not leave any hint on his social media. As a result, one could not tell with certainty if he is married, just engaged or single.

    Johnny Dutch Family

    Johnny Dutch’s mother Deborah Dutch, age 60, is a program support analyst at Trident Systems in Clayton, North Carolina.

    On social media, Johnny has mentioned how his mother would be thrilled and proud of him winning on the reality show.

    And so far, that is all that Johhny had to say about his family.

    Johnny Dutch Height

    Johhny Dutch stands around 1.8 meters tall in height.

    Is Johnny Dutch On Instagram?

    Yes. Johnny Dutch is on Instagram and until 18 December 2021, the account @dutchfilmmaker had 27 posts and
    4,800 followers.

    Johnny could be found also on Twitter @DutchFilmmaker and on FAcebook @TheJohnnyDutch.

    Related FAQs

    • Where Is Johnny Dutch From?

    Johnny Dutch was born and raised in Raleigh in North Carolina. Although he says that when he is ready for a change of scenery one day, he plans to live in Amsterdam, Netherlands stating “I love that city!”

    • What Is Johnny Dutch’s Job Beyond Stripping?

    As per LinkedIn, Johnny Dutch has been a cinematographer and film editor for more than ten years.

    He has been a founder and director of A Dutch Production Film Company in Atlanta, Georgia, as of August 2012.

    Before that, for three years, he was an audio grip operator and freelance videographer at IronWorx Media.

    For a year and half, Johnny worked as a full-time substitute teacher at Wake County Public School.

    Johnny attended Clayton High School before making his way to Columbia and the University of South Carolina, where he earned a degree in Media Arts, specializing in Film Production.

    All through high school and college, Johnny was into sports especially into fields and track. His bio read: Johnny’s success started early on the track. And while at Clayton, he was a nine-time State Champion, named All-American every year, and a two-time Nike Nationals Champion (2006 Outdoors & 2007 Indoors).

    At 19, he was 5th at the 2008 Olympic Trials, three-tenths on a second from making the team.

    This way Johnny went onto  holds a top-5 rank in the world and has represented Team USA at the World Championships in 2009 and 2015. However, the next year a serious leg injury during olympic trial caused a forever halt on his Olympics dreams.

    Still, even today, Johnny is known as the “Spike Lee” of Track & Field who in some way continues to stay in the sports scene. So, when he is doing his independent filmmaking on YouTube, he helps athletes to bring their ideas into life.

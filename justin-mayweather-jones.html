    Justin Mayweather Jones is the welterweight boxer. He is popularly known as the half brother of former professional legend boxer Floyd Mayweather.

    Floyd Mayweather, a second-best boxer of the 21st century

    Floyd Mayweather is recognized as the best defensive boxer in history. 

    He has set several records with his outstanding performance, including 26 consecutive wins in the world title fights. 

    He is one of the most remunerative pay-per-view attractions of all time in any sports and was the world’s highest-paid athlete. 

    The renowned boxer was named the ‘Fighter of the Decade’ in 2010 by the Boxing Association of America’. 

    He has won multiple awards during his fighting days, including The Ring Magazine’s Fighter of the Year award, BWAA Fighter of the year award.

    Recently in 2020, as per Ranker’s list, he is the second-best boxers of the 21st century. Similarly, he is also ranked as 2nd greatest boxer of all time, pound for pound by BoxRec. 

    After setting an exceptional record, he has taken retirement and worked as a boxing promoter in his founded firm, Mayweather Promotions.

    Wiki: Boxing career and victories

    Since his amateur days, Justin had shown outstanding boxing performance and had won the 152-pound novice level at the Michigan Golden Gloves Tournament.

    He started professionally fighting in the boxing ring in 2013, where he won against Rick Ogden. 

    After that, he took a short break and resumed in 2016, where he won against Albert le III in “Billar EI Perro Salado,” Tijuana. 

    After winning, he participates in the ‘Young Guns’ and got victory there as well. 

    Seeing his performance and abilities, one of his uncles, Roger Mayweather, who is a professional boxer and a boxing analyst, said that he is ‘A Champion-in-waiting.’

    Apart from boxing, he is also interested in blogging. He has a YouTube channel, ‘The Justin Mayweather Show,’ where he posted videos about various aspects of life and his training session.

    Not married but multiple relationships

    He is not married yet. Nor are there any rumors that he has a girlfriend. 

    However, his brother is famous for his successful boxing career and his multiple relationships. At first, he dated Erica Dixon. 

    After her, his next girlfriend was Josie Harris. He was in live-in-relationship with her for 14 years, from 1993 to 2007.

    After separating from her, he was seen with Melissa Brim for two years. Later on, he was in an affair with Shantel Jackson starting from 2006 to 2015. 

    He was never loyal in his relationship; while dating Jackson, he saw other girls such as Rozonda, Keyshia Cole, Jessica Burciaga, and Princess Love. 

    Currently, he is dating 19 years Doralie Medina. However, as per Distractify.com, he is engaged to Instagram model, Gallienna Nabila, in 2019.

    No wife only girlfriends

    His brother does not have any wife till now despite dating many women. He even shares a total of 4 children from girlfriends. 

    One of the girlfriends from which he is blessed with a daughter, Iyanna Mayweather, is Melissa Brim. 

    At first, he met her at the lounge of a Las Vegas casino when she was only 16 and had a son, Devanna, whom he adopted later. 

    But after the birth of their daughter in 2000, they were not on speaking terms as she accused him of domestic violence. 

    When he apologized to her, they got in touch again and started co-parenting their daughter.

    Raising three children without the wedding

    His next girlfriend, Josie Harris, shares his three children, Koraun, Zio, and Jirah.  

    Unfortunately, she is mo more with him now as she was found dead in a car at her home in March 2020. 

    He was seen paying tribute to her in his Instagram account, mentioning her as his angel, sweetheart, love, rock, family, kiss sunshine, and flowers.

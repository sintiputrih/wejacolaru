    25 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Kylie and Kendall Jenner showed their unique-yet-complimentary sister style while celebrating Kim Kardashian's birthday.

    The sisters both attended their sister's 43rd birthday dinner at Funke in Beverly Hills, where they wore elevated versions of some classic nightlife looks. For the special occasion, the 818 Tequila founder opting for a long-sleeved midi-length dress, featuring a skintight silhouette made of leopard print fabric with a glossy, geometric overlay. She also channeled the high-fashion flip-flop trend in black sandals with a single toe strap attached to the ankle strap by a gold chain.

    She completed the outfit with a pair of oversized tortoise-shell sunglassess, while wearing her hair in loose waves with a middle part. Her glam look included a light pink lip and inky black nails.

    Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    The Kylie Cosmetics mogul championed the dominance of the little black dress, choosing a leather zip-up tube dress with added pockets and zipper detailing. She styled the piece with sheer black tights, black strappy sandals with a low heel, a small black handbag, and black oval sunglasses, going sans jewelry. She also wore her hair in light waves paired with a simple pink lip.

    Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    The famous sisters both attended Paris Fashion week late last month, where they took over the City of Lights with a marathon of stunning looks. Kendall's most notable looks applied her elegant It-girl spin to more subdued palettes, including a beige trenchcoat worn as a minidress for Gucci's runway show, and a sculptural dark brown Bottega Venetta midi dress. Meanwhile, Kylie made a truly showstopping appearance at Schiaparelli's show, where she wore a glittering sequined gown with a literal keyhole-shaped cutout at the center of her bust.

    Career

    Kenny Omega made his professional wrestling debut with Premier Championship Wrestling in the early 2000s. A year later, he won gold in the company. Omega worked for several independent promotions throughout his career, earning him the title of “Best Wrestler Ever.” Omega made his PWG debut in 2008, winning the PWG World Championship at the Battle of Los Angeles event.

    He defeated Ryusuke Taguchi at Wrestle Kingdom 9 to win the IWGP Junior Heavyweight Championship for the first time. The stable turned on AJ Styles after Kingdom match 10 and crowned Omega as their leader. Omega defeated Hiroshi Tanahashi at The New Beginning in Niigata to win the IWGP Intercontinental Championship. He and The Young Bucks won the NEVER Open weight 6-Man Tag Team Championship less than a week later.

    After defeating Michael Elgin, Jay Lethal, and Tomohiro Ishii in an eight-man tournament, Omega has crowned the inaugural IWGP United States Champion at G1 Special. Omega began working with Adam Page, a hangman. On Jericho’s cruise, they defeated SCU for the AEW World Tag Team Championship. They faced a number of opponents prior to their split as a result of Page’s storyline alcoholism. At All Out 2020, the duo lost the AEW World Tag Team Championship to FTR.

    Omega has been the Impact World Champion since 2020, when he defeated Rich Swann. Christian Cage dethroned him after a few months. Omega faced a number of challenges during his reign as champion. Omega became embroiled in a feud with Hangman Adam Page.
